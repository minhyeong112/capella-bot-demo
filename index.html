The World's 50 Best Hotels 2024 - No. 1

CAPELLA BANGKOK

Let your heart meander the river.

With panoramic views of the majestic Chao Phraya River, Capella Bangkok, among the luxury five-star hotels in Bangkok, Thailand, is a low-rise riverside gateway to the eternal beauty of nature and the urban oasis of the city. Inspired by the soul of riverfront life, this intimate sanctuary features 101 suites and villas with views of the river, offering both privacy and immersion in its privileged setting. Imbued with the personality of a boutique pied-à-terre, this philosophy extends across all elements, from contemporary dining to the renowned Auriga Wellness.

Resort Programming

  • Capella Bangkok

Accommodation

Our exquisite suites and villas are all contemporary, calming, sanctuaries that invite the beauty of the Thai capital’s riverfront to work as their centerpiece. Our luxury accommodation in Bangkok, including 7 villas on the river’s edge, offers panoramic views of the river with either a private balcony or a verandah with a jacuzzi plunge pool.

  • ROOMS
  • SUITES

ROOMS

Spacious residentially styled guestrooms in the low-rise building starting from 61 sqm., each with floor-to-ceiling windows and outdoor living spaces from which to enjoy a front-row seat to the spectacle of the Chao Phraya river. 

Discover More
SUITES

Exquisite suites in the low-rise building ranging from 95 to 209 sqm., are equipped with separate living rooms, sunlit bathrooms and spacious balconies of which to delight in a serene breakfast or a decadent glass of Champagne at sunset.

Discover More
VILLA

Intimate Villas by the river including a two-bedroom Presidential Villa, all boasting separate living and dining space with folding glass doors that lead to lush private gardens. Each Villa on the ground floor is equipped with an outdoor jacuzzi plunge pool, a riverfront daybed and a generous sunlit bathroom with an oversized tub, indoor and outdoor showers.
